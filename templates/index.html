  1. Dashboard - Citi Cycles

Citi Bike is the nation's largest bike share program, with 12,000 bikes and 750 stations across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens and Jersey City. It was designed for quick trips with convenience in mind, and it’s a fun and affordable way to get around town.


Riding a Citi bike is as easy as 1, 2, 3

  • FInd a bike station and unlock a bike using your daily ride code or member key
  • Take as many rides as you like while your pass or membership is active.
  • Return the bike to any of the 750 stations.

Which stations are most popular? Has the proportion of short-term customers and annual subscribers changed? What is the gender breakdown of active participants (Male v. Female)? How does the average trip duration change by age? The data collected by Citibike in the following areas helps answer these and other questions.

  1. Trip Duration (seconds)
  2. Start Time and Date
  3. Stop Time and Date
  4. Start Station Name
  5. End Station Name
  6. Station ID
  7. User Type (Customer = 24-hour pass or 3-day pass user; Subscriber = Annual Member)
  8. Gender
  9. Year of Birth
